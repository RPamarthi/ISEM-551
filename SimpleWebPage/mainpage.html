Share-N-Care is an e-portal for the students of Harrisburg University of Science and Technology, which provides a platform for exchange of information on sharing the students' needs. This facilitates the students to share their needs, exchange the information. This online media will help the students in posting their requirements or information which will be accessible by all the students and those who are interested in that information can respond to it.
